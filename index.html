The Formula Racing series and all about them

In this webpage, we will be looking into the Formula racing series and how to get to F1.


Go Karting



Alright. The first stage to getting to a profesional Racing series, such as F1, is go karting.
In order so start your go karting journey, you have to start doing rentals go karting. Basically, for part of the go karting stage, all you have to do is go to your local go karting track that allows you to borrow one of thier go karts
After 1-2 years of this, you need to look for a team to sponsor you/let you joing them.
But first, (depending on the karting Host you are trying to race in) you need to obtain a racing licence. To obtain his licence, you must contact your country's nation sporting authority (like karting australia or EMSO) to find out specific requirments and apply online or in person.
You gnerally need to complete an application, pass a medical examination, and may have to complete a written and/or practical test, depending on your age and experience.
requirements vary by country and licence type, so always check with the issuing authority first.
