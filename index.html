The Formula Racing series and all about them

In this webpage, we will be looking into the Formula racing series and how to get to F1.


Go Karting



Alright. The first stage to getting to a profesional Racing series, such as F1, is go karting.
In order so start your go karting journey, you have to start doing rentals go karting. Basically, for part of the go karting stage, all you have to do is go to your local go karting track that allows you to borrow on of thier go karts
After 1-2 years of this, you need to look for a team to sponsor you/let you joing them.
But first, (depending on the karting Host you are trying to race in) you need to obtain a racing licence
